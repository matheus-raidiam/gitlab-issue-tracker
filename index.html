Open Finance Brasil - GitLab Issues SLA Dashboard

This dashboard pulls issues from the Open Finance GitLab project and gives a quick view of SLA risk, activity, and context. It calculates working days and applies SLAs based on tags (Nature, Platform, Product, Working Group, and Status). Use filters, sorting, and local notes to triage faster.
